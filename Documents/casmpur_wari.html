🏫 Campus Wari

"Connecting surplus to demand, one campus at a time."

A platform where college students can share resources, host services, offer guidance, or just hang out — all within the safety and familiarity of their own campus.

🤝 Collaborate 📚 Share Notes 🎓 Find Tutors ☕ Hang Out

🤔 Why Build This?

🎯

Meta Goal: Learn by Doing

We're learning full-stack development. This project is small enough to manage but big enough to learn real-world skills — databases, APIs, frontend, deployment.

🌱

Campus as a Constraint

Limiting to our college keeps things manageable. It creates a dense, trusted network where real connections happen.

💡

Set an Example

We want future creators to see: "They built this as students. So can we." A concrete anchor for the college dev community.

🔐 Trust & Logistics — Solved by Campus

Existing platforms have friction. Campus Wari removes it naturally.

Problem How Campus Solves It
"Is this person legit?" ✅ Same college, likely mutual friends
"Where do we meet?" ✅ Library, cafeteria, common room — obvious!
"Is this still active?" ✅ Student density = quick matches & responses
Ghosting / No-shows ✅ Way less likely when you might see them in class

🧠 Our Philosophy

Yes, Facebook and Instagram exist. But we wanted:

🏗️

Something of Our Own

A platform built for students by students — not a generic social network.

🖼️

A Concrete Example

An anchor project that says: "You can build things too."

🔄

Surplus → Demand

Everyone has something to give (knowledge, notes, time) and something they need. We connect them.

📖 Two Stories, One Mission

— That's where we come in. 🎓

"Anjali has handmade notes from last semester. Useless to her, but an absolute treasure for Thajaba. How do they connect and share this?"

— That's where we come in. 📝

We announce, connect, and help people find collaborators.
